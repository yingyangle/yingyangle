Visualization Labs


For my visualization course, I created a number of interactive visualizations primarily using d3. These labs were designed for us to practice our technical skills and fluency in d3, so each lab is relatively simple and focuses on certain techniques and capabilites of d3 such as scales and axes, update pattern, brush and zoom, animated transitions, and maps.




Visualization Mini Projects


A major part of visualization is cohesive and effective storytelling. In my visualization class, we collaborated as a group on mini projects to create communicative and interactive visualizations exploring datasets of personal interest to us. We also practiced analyzing and redesigning visualizations using the principles of design and visual encoding we talked about in class. For these projects, we used d3 and Tableau to create our visualizations.
